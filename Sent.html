Youtube Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment Analysis was done on comments extracted from youtube
Sentiment Analysis

Summary

Data retrieval from YouTube was facilitated through the YouTube API, capturing essential video statistics and comments for analysis. The collected data underwent rigorous cleaning processes utilizing data-cleaning techniques, including the utilization of powerful tools such as Regular Expressions (RegEx). This meticulous cleaning process ensured the extraction of high-quality data, free from inconsistencies or inaccuracies. To extract insightful sentiment information from the comments associated with different movies, VADER (Valence Aware Dictionary and sentiment Reasoner) Sentiment Analysis was employed. This analysis method, integrated with the NLTK (Natural Language Toolkit) library, enabled a granular understanding of the sentiments expressed within the comments. Furthermore, to enhance the text-processing capabilities and ensure robust analysis, additional text-preprocessing techniques were implemented. Libraries like spaCy and NLTK were utilized for tasks such as tokenization, stemming, and lemmatization, which collectively contributed to the extraction of more nuanced insights from the textual data. This comprehensive approach, involving advanced tools and libraries, allowed for a thorough examination of YouTube data, enabling the extraction of valuable sentiment-based insights for various movies.
